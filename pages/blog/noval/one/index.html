梅尔罗德 Melrod

——城市暗影下的正义与真相 — Justice and Truth beneath the City's Shadows
2025-04-01
小说
犯罪悬疑
HealthJian

在梅罗德这座看似繁华的城市背后，实则暗流涌动，危机四伏。

Behind the seemingly prosperous city of Melrod, there are undercurrents and dangers.

平静被一声巨响打破，梅罗德港口突发爆炸，瞬间火光冲天，照亮了黑夜。身为警察却伪装成记者的卢卡斯，本在执行秘密任务，却因这场爆炸被卷入更大的漩涡。

The calm was broken by a loud noise, a sudden explosion at Melrod port, with flames instantly lighting up the night sky. Lucas, a police officer disguised as a journalist, was on a secret mission but got dragged into a bigger whirlpool because of this explosion.

港口三区警局分队遇袭，激烈的枪战爆发，生死瞬间交错。卢卡斯与搭档安德利临危受命，投身于枪林弹雨之中。然而，局势愈发复杂，南门出现神秘的武装人员，集装箱里惊现烧焦的残骸，每一个线索都指向更深的阴谋。

The Port District 3 police squad was ambushed, a fierce gunfight erupted, and life and death intertwined in an instant. Lucas and his partner Andrey were commissioned in the crisis, plunging into the hail of bullets. However, the situation became increasingly complex, with mysterious armed personnel appearing at the south gate and charred remains found in a container, each clue pointing to a deeper conspiracy.

在真相与谎言交织的城市里，唯有正义才能照亮黑暗的角落。

In a city where truth and lies intertwine, only justice can illuminate the dark corners.

随着调查的深入，更多的人物浮出水面。检察官、警长、联邦安全局人员纷纷登场，各怀心思，为了权力、利益或是正义，展开了一场惊心动魄的博弈。

As the investigation deepened, more characters emerged. Prosecutors, sheriffs, and Federal Security Bureau personnel appeared one after another, each with their own intentions, engaging in a thrilling game for power, profit, or justice.

在这混乱的局势中，卢卡斯结识了美丽而神秘的艾琳。他们的爱情在血雨腥风中悄然萌芽，却不知艾琳的父亲卡尔，竟与案件有着千丝万缕的联系。

In this chaotic situation, Lucas met the beautiful and mysterious Erin. Their love quietly sprouted amidst the bloodshed, unaware that Erin's father, Carl, had intricate connections to the case.

爆炸背后的黑手究竟是谁？是隐藏在警局内部的腐败势力，还是另有其人？卢卡斯能否在这错综复杂的局面中揭开真相，又能否守护住自己的爱情？

Who is the mastermind behind the explosion? Is it the corrupt forces hidden within the police station, or someone else? Can Lucas uncover the truth in this complex situation, and can he protect his love?

《梅尔罗德》，带你走进这座危机四伏的城市，一同探寻真相与正义的答案。

"Melrod" takes you into this perilous city to explore the answers to truth and justice together.
